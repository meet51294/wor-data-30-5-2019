About Us. House Painting Has Never Been This Good!

Looking for reliable and professional house painters? Herbie's Painting is the right choice! Herbie is certified painter with over 20 yrs of experience

Herbie's Painting is one of the most popular house painting companies in the Lower Mainland and the Fraser Valley, Canada. When it comes to exceptional exterior, interior house painting, no one does it better than Herbie's Painting. We also attend to your residential, industrial and commercial painting needs. We are proud to serve places like Fraser Valley and in Lower Mainland areas.

Herbie’s Painting has over 20 years of painting experience and is familiar with many residential neighborhoods. Quite often homeowners can have a quotation over the telephone. A typical house exterior or interior can be completed at promise days with a crew of professional painters. The professional painters at Herbie’s Painting also paint commercial buildings, industrial complexes, basements, apartments, condominiums and other residential dwellings.

You can guarantee at Herbie’s Painting with our quality services, Painting Has Never Been This Good!
